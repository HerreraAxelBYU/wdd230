Spaces and Capacities


The Temple Inn & Suites gives you the option of holding your celebration on an outdoor terrace that accommodates up to 300 people or in its covered room for between 100 and 250 people. In its facilities you will find:

  • Living room
  • Dance floor
  • Yard
  • Terrace
  • Differentiated bathrooms
  • Own parking
